# What it is

Boomi Companion is an AI development partner integrated with the Boomi platform. Developers work with it through natural-language conversation and by inspecting the integration artifacts it produces on the platform canvas.

# How developers use it

Two modes, usually blended in the same session:

- [Conversational mode](/concepts/conversational-mode.md) for scoping, planning, and iterating on intent.
- [Canvas mode](/concepts/canvas-mode.md) for inspecting the process, opening map steps, reviewing groovy scripts, and running test executions.

# What it draws on

- [Skill folders](/concepts/skill-folders.md) that describe how the Boomi platform works.
- The [connector registry](/concepts/connector-registry.md), which defines what Companion can integrate with in your tenant.

# Working posture

The course frames the developer as a *co-creator* with Companion. The good sessions look like:

1. Scope the request the way a senior developer would. See [planning together](/modules/04-planning-together.md).
2. Open a conversation with Companion that describes intent and asks what Companion needs from you.
3. Let Companion produce a plan and a process.
4. Move to the canvas to verify the map steps, scripts, and connectors.
5. Test.
6. Close with a [reflection](/modules/05-stopping.md) so the next session compounds.
