# Co-Creating with Boomi Companion — Knowledge Bundle

This bundle captures the knowledge from the Boomi L&D course *Co-Creating with Boomi Companion* in Open Knowledge Format v0.1. The source is a SCORM 1.2 package built in Articulate Rise. This bundle re-represents the course's concepts, scenarios, and modules as agent- and human-readable markdown so the content can be searched, cross-linked, and reused outside an LMS.

# Contents

* [Course overview](/course.md) - top-level concept describing the course and its learning intent
* [Modules](/modules/) - the ordered learning modules that make up the course
* [Concepts](/concepts/) - the underlying platform and product concepts the course teaches
* [Scenarios](/scenarios/) - narrated video scenarios embedded in the course
* [References](/references/) - source materials and provenance
